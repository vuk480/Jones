About Us
About Us

Nestled in the heart of California, we are a dedicated team who live and breathe the thrill of carving through snow-covered slopes. Founded in 2010, "Jones" was born out of a shared love for snowboarding. What started as a small group of friends evolved into a full-fledged company committed to bringing the joy of snowboarding to enthusiasts of all levels.

Our Mission At Jones Snowboards, our mission is clear: to elevate your snowboarding experience. We are dedicated to providing top-tier gear, expert guidance, and fostering a community where riders of all levels can share their passion.
